How to Use

Capture Flow

Tap the shutter or pick from Library. Review the photo, choose a mode and/or type a prompt, then tap Send.

Modes

SimpsonsDefault. Cartoon style transfer.
OCRRead text. Creates Todoist tasks (supports lists), email, calendar.
VideoFast 1080p video (LTX). 10s default.
CinemaCinematic video (Kling v3). 10s default.

Duration

shortvideo 6s, cinema 5s
longvideo 20s, cinema 30s

Type "long cinema" or "short video" in text box.

Tips

Text box overrides mode buttons. Handwritten lists become multiple Todoist tasks. All results are texted to your phone. Camera pauses when you switch to Queue.
