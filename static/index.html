How to Use

Getting Started

Triple-tap the shutter to activate the camera. After capture, the camera turns off automatically. Pick from gallery anytime.

Modes

SimpsonsDefault. Turns your photo into a Simpsons cartoon.
OCRReads text from image. Creates Todoist task, sends email, adds calendar event if date found.
VideoFast 1080p video from image (LTX). 10s default.
CinemaCinematic video from image (Kling v3). 10s default.

Duration Modifiers

shortShortest: video 6s, cinema 5s
longLongest: video 20s, cinema 30s

Type "long cinema" or "short video" in the text box.

Custom Prompts

Type anything in the text box to override the default. Text box always wins over mode buttons.
